NOVITA'

Two exhibitions have been organized, one from January 18 to February 21, 2013, devoted to the theme of ecology having as title "Environment and life", and one inspired to the problem of violence on women, of title: "Women Killing", from February 22 to April 5 2013"
In this last exhibition, the 8th of March, "Day of Women", an event has been organized, in which the theme of the exhibition has been exposed by a poetic work, "MEDUSA", dramatically declaimed by the author, Tiziana Cera Rosco
